Scope

This policy explains how the App handles information when you use it to estimate Ethiopian personal income tax, rent tax, and commercial tax.

What data we collect

  • User-entered figures: salary, rent amount, revenue, pension rate, etc. We do not collect, store, or transmit these values. They are processed locally on your device to produce calculations.
  • No personal information: The App does not ask for your name, email, phone number, ID, or address.

How data is processed

All calculations occur on-device. We do not operate a backend server for your inputs. If you clear the app data or uninstall the App, locally stored history (if you enabled it) is removed from your device.

Analytics, crash reporting, and ads

We do not use third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs in this App. We do not access the Google Advertising ID.

Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user-entered data with third parties. If third-party services are integrated (see the optional section above), they may receive limited technical data per their policies.

Data retention

We do not retain your inputs on our servers. Any optional history stored by the App remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall.

Security

Because your inputs are processed on-device and not transmitted to a server, risks related to transmission are minimized. Always keep your device OS updated and secured.

Children’s privacy

The App is intended for general audiences and does not target children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • You can use the App without providing personal information.
  • You can clear local history in the App (if enabled) or uninstall the App at any time.

We aim to keep figures current, but tax laws may change. Please verify with official government resources.
